Anatomy of a sign

One letter.
Four layers of craft.

01

Acrylic face

UV-stable cast acrylic, CNC-cut to the exact curve of your logotype. This is the color the street sees.

02

LED module array

Hand-spaced modules for an even, hotspot-free glow — bright at night, subtle by day.

03

Aluminum returns

Bent and welded side walls that give the letter its depth and keep weather out for years.

04

Weatherproof backplate

Sealed backing with hidden mounts and cabling — engineered for Cavite sun, rain and wind.

What we do

How it happens

From sketch to switch-on.

01

Consult & site visit

We measure your façade, check power and mounting points, and talk through what your brand needs to say from the road.

02

Design & mockup

You get a scaled visual of the sign on a photo of your actual storefront — approve it before we cut a single sheet.

03

Fabrication

Acrylic, aluminum, steel and LEDs come together in our Silang workshop. Every layer you saw above, built by hand.

04

Install & light up

Our crew mounts, wires and tests on site — then hands you the switch for the first glow.
